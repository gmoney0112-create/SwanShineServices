Frequently asked questions

The original support page had the right idea but some encoding damage. This version keeps the purpose simple and answers the questions most likely to block a booking.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes. The default assumption is that Swan Shine arrives with the supplies needed for the service scope unless a client requests something specific.

Can I request recurring cleaning?

Yes. Weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly frequencies are built directly into the booking flow, and the confirmation page now reinforces recurring follow-up.

How accurate is the estimate?

The estimate is a useful starting range based on service type, size, and frequency. Final pricing can shift once access, condition, and special requests are confirmed.

Do you handle move-out cleaning?

Yes. Move-in and move-out cleaning is a featured service on the homepage and inside the booking modal.

What areas do you serve?

The current site focuses on San Antonio and surrounding neighborhoods such as Stone Oak, Alamo Heights, Downtown, the Northwest Side, and the Medical Center area.
