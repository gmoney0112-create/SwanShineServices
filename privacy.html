Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

What we collect

When you request a booking or estimate through our website, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Service address
  • Service preferences (service type, preferred date/time, estimate range, and any notes you provide)

How we use your information

We use the information you submit to schedule services, communicate with you about your request, and deliver cleaning services.

Third-party tools

  • GoHighLevel (CRM) — used to receive and manage booking requests and communicate with customers.
  • GitHub Pages — used to host this website.

SMS messaging

By providing your phone number and submitting a booking or estimate request, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Swan Shine Cleaning Service. These messages may include:

  • Booking confirmations and scheduling updates
  • Estimate follow-ups and service reminders
  • Responses to your inquiries

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any SMS message at any time. You will receive one confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

For help: Reply HELP to any SMS message or contact us at mary@inspirebuildmotivate.com.

We do not sell your data

We do not sell your personal information — including your phone number or SMS consent — to third parties.

Data requests

If you would like to request access to or deletion of your submitted data, contact us at mary@inspirebuildmotivate.com.
