Swan Shine Cleaning

Privacy policy

Effective date: May 14, 2026

Swan Shine Cleaning collects the information a visitor submits through the booking form so the business can respond to estimate requests, confirm appointments, and communicate about services.

What we collect

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Address and preferred booking timing
  • Service details such as frequency, size, and notes

How we use it

  • To respond to estimate and booking requests
  • To confirm scheduling and service details
  • To follow up by phone, email, or SMS about the requested service

How form data is handled

The site currently submits booking data to the configured CRM webhook endpoint. The homepage is now prepared for a future serverless proxy if you want to hide the raw webhook URL and add stricter server-side validation.

Cookies and analytics

The site can optionally load Google Analytics 4 when a real measurement ID is configured. Local browser storage is also used to save a draft booking form and apply lightweight rate limiting against repeat submissions.
