Hi, I'm Mary.

I started Swan Shine because I kept hearing the same thing from San Antonio homeowners: they didn't trust the last service they hired. Either someone didn't show up, something went missing, or the clean just wasn't what they expected.

I built Swan Shine around one simple idea: show up, do the work right, and treat every home like it's our own. Five years later, that's still the standard we hold ourselves to — on every job, every visit.

Every cleaner on our team is background-checked, trained, and personally vouched for. We use eco-friendly products because our clients have kids and pets. And if anything is ever less than perfect, we come back and fix it — no charge. That's the Swan Shine promise.
